A family-friendly indoor play space in Cartersville, Georgia, designed for infants through early elementary-aged children. We offer open play, birthday parties, memberships, field trips, fundraisers, and private events in a clean, safe, and engaging environment.

Designed for Little Explorers

Our play space is thoughtfully designed for children ages 6 months through 8 years. Every play zone is age-appropriate with soft surfaces, rounded edges, and sensory-rich activities that encourage motor skill development, imaginative play, and social interaction. From crawling infants to adventurous grade-schoolers, every child finds their perfect play area. One accompanying adult per child is always free.

The Peachy Pals Standard

Cleanliness is at the core of the Peachy Pals experience. We follow a rigorous daily sanitization routine using hospital-grade, child-safe disinfectants on every surface, play structure, and shared toy. High-touch areas are cleaned multiple times throughout the day. Grippy socks are required for all guests to maintain hygiene and prevent slips. Our staff is First Aid & CPR certified, and we maintain strict capacity limits so your child always plays in a safe, supervised environment.

Unforgettable Celebrations

Let us handle everything so you can enjoy the party! Choose from our Just Peachy, Peachy Pal, or Feeling Peachy Deluxe packages. Every party includes a 2-hour time block with dedicated play time, a private party area, drinks and snacks for each child, and complete setup and cleanup by our team. Upgrade for goodie bags, a birthday shirt for the guest of honor, and ice cream. Parties are available any day of the week — weekday rates start at just $229.
